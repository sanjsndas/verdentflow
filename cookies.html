Effective Date: June 2024

Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how VerdantFlow Irrigation Services ("we," "us," "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and services. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help enhance your browsing experience and provide us with information about how our website is used.

Understanding how and why we use cookies will empower you to make informed choices about your data and privacy as you use our site.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are text files that contain small pieces of data sent from a website and stored by your web browser on your device. They enable websites to remember your preferences, login status, and other useful information for a better user experience.

Cookies can also help website owners understand how visitors interact with their site by collecting anonymous data on site traffic and engagement.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the operation of our website and enable you to use key features such as secure areas and sessions.

2.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect anonymous information about how visitors use our site, which pages are most popular, and whether visitors receive error messages. We use this data to improve our site's performance and your experience.

2.3 Functionality Cookies

Functionality cookies allow our site to remember choices you make, like your language preference or location, providing enhanced functionality and personalization.

2.4 Advertising and Targeting Cookies

These cookies track your browsing habits to personalize advertising content that may be of interest to you. We currently do not use advertising cookies ourselves but may allow third-party ads to serve you.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us provide core functionalities, remember your settings, improve performance, analyze site usage, and personalize content and services.

We use cookies to:

  • Recognize you when you return to our site
  • Enhance site navigation and usability
  • Measure site performance and usage metrics
  • Customize content and communications based on your preferences

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party providers like Google Analytics to place cookies on our site to gather anonymous usage data to help us improve our services. These cookies are governed by the respective third party's privacy policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You have control over how cookies are used on your device. Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

For more information on managing cookies, visit your browser’s help section or https://www.allaboutcookies.org.

6. Cookie Consent Dialog

To comply with privacy regulations such as GDPR, we display a cookie consent dialog at the bottom of the screen on your first visit, allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies.

Your choice will be saved and respected during your visits to our site.

7. Data Security

We maintain strict security measures to protect the data collected by cookies and ensure your privacy.

8. Your Rights And Choices

You have the right to withdraw your consent to the use of non-essential cookies at any time via our cookie consent dialog or your browser settings.

You may also request information about the data that we collect or ask us to delete your data within the bounds of applicable laws.

9. Updates To This Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in how we use cookies or applicable laws. The updated policy will be posted here with the revision date.
